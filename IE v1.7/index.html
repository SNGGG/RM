Millenials are the people born between 1981 and 1996, being the generation that grew up surrounded by technology and social networks it was easier for them to see everything that was happening around the world opening their mind and increasing their tolerance. Millennials are basically the same as other generations in their age group, with only slight differences. They may be more international, more diverse and more ambitiou. However, Millenials are the most stress generation in the world; worrying about work, finances, studies and relationships is taking a toll on us mentally and physically.
